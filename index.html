Adopt A Rescue Cat

Over 1.2 million cats go into UK animal shelters each year.
Can you give them the home they deserve?

Why adopt a rescue cat?

Cats are adventurous, loveable and intelligent animals. They act as a source of stress relief and are an excellent addition to any family home. Just like humans, they have their own personalities; some might might be outgoing, while others just like to stay inside with their owners. Cats are usually rehomed in pairs, which means you'll have two fun new additions to your home. Lively kittens are a great addition to a family, but older cats tend to be more relaxed and good for families with older kids. Cats are very sociable creatures that can bring joy and fun to your life.

How else can I help?

If you are unable to home a cat, then you can donate your time and money to animal shelters near you. Many rescue centres rely on the support of volunteers to stay open. You should also report any stray or homeless cats in your area. Many shelters will come and scan a stray cat, to see if it has an owner.
